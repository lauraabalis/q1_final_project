Recipe Generator

Instructions

1. Would you like to eat breakfast, lunch, dinner, dessert, or a snack? Go to the dropdown menu, and select the meal you wish to eat.

2. What would you like the main ingredient of your recipe to be? Select that ingredient, type it in the box, and click "Find Recipes."

3. Watch each video, and select the recipe that most appeals to you.

4. Rewatch the video that depicts your desired recipe while preparing your meal.
